COMMON QUESTIONS

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to questions we hear most often

Do I need to have previous Pilates experience?

Not at all! Our classes are designed for all levels. We offer beginner-friendly classes and our instructors provide modifications to ensure everyone can participate safely and effectively.

What should I wear to class?

Wear comfortable, fitted athletic wear that allows you to move freely. Grippy socks are required for reformer classes (available for purchase at the studio). For mat classes, barefoot is fine.

How do I book a class?

You can book classes directly through our website by clicking "Book a Class" in the navigation, or through our mobile app. First-time clients receive 20% off their first class pack!

What is your cancellation policy?

We require 12-hour notice for cancellations. Late cancellations (within 12 hours) or no-shows will result in the loss of that class credit. Private sessions require 24-hour notice.

Do you offer private sessions?

Yes! We offer private one-on-one sessions and duet sessions for two. These are perfect for beginners, those with specific goals, or anyone wanting personalized attention.

Is there parking available?

Yes, we have dedicated parking available for our clients. There's also street parking nearby if needed.
