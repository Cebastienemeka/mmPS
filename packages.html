COMMON QUESTIONS

Package FAQs

Can I share my class pack with friends?

Yes! Our 5-class and 10-class packs are shareable with friends and family. Drop-in passes are non-transferable. When booking, simply specify who will be attending each class.

What happens if I don't use all my classes before expiration?

Unused classes expire after the validity period and cannot be refunded. We recommend planning your classes regularly. Extensions may be granted for medical reasons with proper documentation.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require 12 hours notice for cancellations. Late cancellations or no-shows will result in the loss of that class credit from your pack. Private sessions require 24-hour notice.

Can I upgrade from a smaller pack to a larger one?

Yes! You can upgrade at any time by paying the difference in price. The remaining classes from your current pack will be added to your new pack with the extended validity period.

Do you offer refunds on class packs?

Class packs are non-refundable but can be transferred to another person. If you have a medical condition preventing participation, we may offer extensions with a doctor's note.
