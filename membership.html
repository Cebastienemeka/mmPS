MEMBERSHIP FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my membership?

To cancel your membership, please provide 30 days written notice via email to hello@mmpilates.com. Your membership will remain active for the remainder of the billing cycle. No refunds for partial months.

Can I freeze my membership?

Yes! Monthly members can freeze once per year for up to 30 days with 7 days notice. Freeze periods can be extended for medical reasons with doctor's note. No freeze fees apply.

What happens if I miss a class?

We require 12-hour cancellation notice. Late cancellations count as a used class. Three no-shows within a 30-day period may result in temporary booking restrictions.

Can I bring a guest to class?

Monthly members receive one complimentary guest pass per month. Additional guests can be added for the drop-in rate of ₦12,000. Guests must sign a waiver before class.

What's included in my membership?

Your membership includes unlimited classes, priority booking, free mat and towel rental, 10% off workshops, one monthly guest pass, and 15% off MM Pilates merchandise.

Can I switch between monthly and yearly plans?

Yes! You can upgrade from monthly to yearly at any time. The yearly plan offers 20% savings and additional benefits. Downgrades require 30-day notice and take effect at next billing cycle.
